Welcome to the city revolution

The reputation of your place is all-important

PlacesBrands was born from a simple observation. When discovering new cities and countries, expectations and reality do not always match up. How are our perceptions of places formed? And once formed, how can these be shifted? Stories play a big part in shaping our opinions of places. Those who are adept at telling their own story tend to be more successful. Finding the story takes effort, because it has to be rooted in reality. There is no point trying to build a reputation as a party town when your bars all close at 8pm. Developing an identity is a strategic process that unfolds over time. It all starts by discovering the story. There are no short cuts.
